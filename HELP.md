# Thought Bag Help

Thought Bag is a soft little space for catching words and ideas that appear in conversation.  
It is meant to feel lighter than a note app and looser than a formal mind map.

## Add a Keyword

Type a word or short phrase into the input box, then press `Cmd + Enter`.

## Add Floating Text

Type a note, label, or fragment into the input box, then press `Shift + Cmd + Enter`.

Floating text stays as plain text on the canvas instead of becoming a balloon.  
Use it for small notes, context, or words you want to keep visible without grouping them.

## Edit Floating Text

Double-click floating text to edit it.  
Press `Cmd + Enter` to finish editing, or press `Escape` to cancel.

If you finish editing with empty text, the floating text is removed.

## Delete Floating Text

Click floating text to select it, then press `Delete` or `Backspace`.

## Move a Balloon

Drag a small or large balloon to place it where it feels right.

Floating text can also be dragged around freely.

## Lock a Balloon

Right-click a balloon to lock it in place.  
Locked balloons cannot be dragged or moved.  
Right-click again to unlock.  
Locked balloons appear slightly more angular.

Floating text can also be locked and unlocked with right-click.

## Group Balloons

Draw a loose line around multiple balloons to gather them into one larger group balloon.

## Ungroup

Click the `×` on a group balloon to turn it back into small balloons.

## Change Colors

Click a balloon to open the color palette, then choose a color.

## Save / Open Archive

Use `Save Archive` to download your current state.  
Use `Open Archive` to bring it back later, including grouped balloons and floating text.

## A Small Note

Thought Bag is not a strict note-taking tool.  
It works best for casual conversation, early ideas, and loose brainstorming.
