Thought Bag Help

Thought Bag is a soft little space for catching words and ideas that appear in conversation. It is lighter than a note app and looser than a formal mind map.

Add a Keyword

Type a word or short phrase into the input box, then press Cmd + Enter.

Move a Balloon

Drag a small or large balloon to place it where it feels right.

Group Balloons

Draw a loose line around multiple balloons to gather them into one larger group balloon.

Ungroup

Click the × on a group balloon to turn it back into small balloons.

Change Colors

Click a balloon to open the color palette, then choose a color.

Save / Open Archive

Use Save Archive to download your current state. Use Open Archive to bring it back later, including grouped balloons.

Start Fresh

If you want to keep what you have now, save an archive first. Reloading the page clears the current balloons and opens a fresh empty canvas.

Thought Bag is not a strict note-taking tool. It works best for casual conversation, early ideas, and loose brainstorming.
